The Problem

Your POS has reports.
It doesn't have answers.

Flowhub, Dutchie, and every other POS gives you static dashboards. Diggory gives you a conversation.

📉

Rigid dashboards miss the signal

Canned reports show you what happened. They can't explain why, or tell you what to do next. You end up exporting to Excel and doing the work yourself.

💸

280E makes every margin point critical

Federal tax treatment under 280E means cannabis operators can't deduct ordinary business expenses. Understanding your true margin by SKU, category, and vendor isn't nice-to-have — it's survival.

👥

Customer data is sitting unused

You have years of transaction history. Lapsed customers, high-value segments, basket trends — all locked in your POS with no easy way to act on it.

The Solution

Diggory connects directly to your POS, keeps your transaction history up to date, and gives you an AI that can actually answer operational questions — not just display charts.

Up and running in minutes.

No data warehouse. No ETL pipeline. No consultants.

Step 02
💬

Type questions the way you'd ask a smart analyst. Margins, trends, customers, inventory gaps — Diggory figures out the right query and runs it against live data.

Capabilities

Everything an operator needs to know.

📊

Sales & Revenue Analytics

Daily, weekly, and trailing-period sales with trend analysis. Hourly breakdowns, day-of-week patterns, and comp periods.

💰

Margin Intelligence

True margin by SKU, brand, category, and vendor. Spot margin compression before it becomes a problem. 280E-aware cost analysis.

Inventory Depth

Days-on-hand by product. Dead stock alerts. Velocity ranking. Know exactly what's moving and what's tying up cash on the shelf.
